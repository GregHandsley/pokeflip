# Condition Grading Rubric (v1.0)

**Goal:** A fast, consistent way to bucket a card into NM / LP / MP / HP / DMG using five yes/no checks.

## Lighting & Prep (do this first)

- Even light (daylight or 5000–6500K), no harsh glare
- Wipe with a microfiber to remove dust
- Inspect at ~30–40 cm; then tilt ~30° to catch holo/surface lines

## The Five Checks (answer Yes/No)

1) **Creases/Bends visible under normal light?**  
2) **Edge whitening visible at arm’s length?**  
3) **Corner dings/nicks?**  
4) **Surface scratches/indents visible under normal light (non-holo areas)?**  
5) **Holo/surface scratches visible without aggressive tilting?**

> If any **writing, water damage, tears, or major creases** → **DMG** immediately.

## Mapping to Grade

- **NM (Near Mint)**  
  - All five checks = **No**  
  - Corners sharp; minimal printing imperfections only visible up close
- **LP (Lightly Played)**  
  - 1–2 “Yes” answers; **no** creases/bends  
  - Minor edge whitening or a few light surface/holo hairlines
- **MP (Moderately Played)**  
  - 3 “Yes” answers; still **no** major creases  
  - Noticeable whitening, corner wear, or multiple scratches
- **HP (Heavily Played)**  
  - 4–5 “Yes” answers **or** a small crease/bend that doesn’t split the card  
  - Significant wear, whitening, scratches, may include minor dirt/staining
- **DMG (Damaged)**  
  - Any tear, water damage, major crease/fold, ink/marker, peeling, or splitting

### Quick Decision Tree

1. Any major crease/tear/water/ink? → **DMG**  
2. Else count “Yes” answers:  
   - 0 → **NM**  
   - 1–2 → **LP**  
   - 3 → **MP**  
   - 4–5 → **HP**

## eBay Wording Mapping (for Item Specifics)

- `NM` → **Near Mint or Better**  
- `LP` → **Lightly Played (LP)**  
- `MP` → **Moderately Played (MP)**  
- `HP` → **Heavily Played (HP)**  
- `DMG` → **Damaged**

> Use these in the **Card Condition** item specific. (You can keep the overall listing “Condition” as Used.)

## Photo Requirements (to back up your grade)

- Front and back, flat, full card in frame
- Show edges and corners clearly (slight zoom if needed)
- For Holo: one extra angled photo if scratches are present
- Use the SKU in filename for traceability

## Notes & Exceptions

- **Holo vs non-holo:** count holo hairlines in checks #4–#5.  
- **Manufacturing lines/print lines:** treat like surface scratches.  
- If in doubt between two grades, choose the **lower** (more conservative) grade and note in description.
